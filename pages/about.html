My name is

Dario Colaciuri

Dario Colaciuri was born on September 9, 1991 in Buenos Aires, Argentina. He studied at the "Manuel de Falla" Conservatory of Music. Parallel to his academic life, he was always musically active creating bands or participating with artists from different music genres. He has participated with great musicians and artists from the scene but is currently creating music content for social networks and working as a ghost producer for different artists.
